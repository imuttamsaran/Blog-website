    Whether you are a wanderlust or travelling for the first time, it is always beneficial to be ready for the trip and carry all the essentials that you might need. A travel checklist ensures that you do not forget out on anything for a smooth trip.

    Here is the list of the 20 travel essentials you should carry while travelling:

    1. Mask



    Carrying a set of masks while traveling in pandemic times is one of the most important travel essentials. It is vital to carry not just one but a set because you cannot use the same mask for a long time. It will help you keep safe from coming in direct contact with the sick people around you and be safe in areas where you can’t social distance.

    2. Sanitizer



    With regard to the pandemic’s present situation, sanitizer has become one of the most indispensable travel essentials. Carrying a sanitizer with you while traveling makes it easy to clean your hands whenever and wherever you want without looking for soap and water. It helps you maintain hygiene at all times.

    3. Shoes



    What’s a trip without comfort? Carrying comfortable shoes on a trip will help prevent foot pain and maintain the posture of your feet. These types of shoes help you prevent any kind of long term damage while you hike or trek. Comfortable shoes will help you stay active while improving circulation.

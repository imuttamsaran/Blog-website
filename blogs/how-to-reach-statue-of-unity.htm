    Statue of Unity is the world's tallest statue - 182 meter, which is located in Gujarat, India.

    The nearest city to the Statue of Unity is Vadodara, Gujarat and is located in Kevadiya district, which is 90km from Vadodara.

    There are mainly 4 options to reach Statue of Unity.
    1) You can hire a taxi from Vadodara (which will cost you around 3000 INR).
    2) You can take public transport from Vadodara bus depot (which will cost you around 120 INR).
    3) There are two trains from Vadodara Junction (which will cost you around 75-1145 INR).
    4) The last option is - Own Vehicles.

    If you are taking public transportations (Bus) it will drop you to the nearest Kevadiya bus depot and you can hire a taxi to the Statue of Unity's official bus depot which will take you the Statue. The bus facility provided free of cost by the government.

    If you are taking a train, it will drop you to the nearest railway station i.e. Ekta Nagar, and from there you can hire a taxi to the Statue of Unity's bus depot, which is around 4km from the station.

    at Ekta Nagar railway station

    At ticket counter you have 3 choices,

  • Standard (150INR)
  • Premium (380INR)
  • Express way (1030INR)

    • [For any kind of ticket there is free of cost bus facility for whole day from ticket counter to various place which I explained bellow]

    If you take the standard price ticket than can you go upto foot area of Statue and also explore the Museum at ground floor

    If you choose the Premium option than you can explore whole statue. You can go upto 153 meter of statue.

    If you take Express way ticket than it will help you to pass all the way queue and save time.

    After visiting statue you can go for

  • Jungle Safari
  • Sardar Sarovar dam
  • Valley of Flower

    • In Jungle Safary there are many types of wild animal are present, so you take a chance to look at them.

    After Jungle Safari visit, your bus will drop you at view point of Sardar Sarovar dam. The Sardar Sarovar dam is Gujarat's highest dam with height of 163m and length of 1210m. This dam produce 1450MW hydropower which is 1 billion kWh every year.

    Sardar Sarovar dam

    After finished exploring the view of dam, your bus will drop you at Valley of flower. This valley also known as Bharat Van. This valley have more than 22,00,000 plants.

    Valley of Flower

    Valley of Flower

    After exploring all the venues you journey to STATUE OF UNITY will be over.

    TIPS:-

    If you are a budget friendly traveller like me than you should carry some food with you for breakfast or lunch, because near the statue or in foodcart, the available food is so much overpriced.
